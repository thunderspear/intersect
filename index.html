Karl J. Lindborg, CPO
Intersect OPS

Navigating new technologies in a rapidly advancing field does not need to be frustrating. Traditional lower limb techniques often create problems when combined with upper limb technology. I've spent the more recent half of my 27 year CPO career specializing in issues unique to upper limb prothetics. In this time, I have developed expertise in reducing upper limb prosthesis rejection rates, navigating difficult coding for upper limb loss, and educating teams on the unique and often subtle differences between solving upper and lower limb problems. Let me help you build the confidence you need to provide the best possible care to your patients.
