Consult

Prosthetic and orthotic technology is rapidly advancing. Most clinicians have minimal exposure to upper limb prosthetics. While they are proficient in lower limb prosthetics, we have seen that lower limb techniques applied to upper limb technology can create problems. For clinicians presented with an upper limb case, consultation with a trusted specialist can make the difference between success and failure of the device, and adoption or rejection by the client. Intersect OPS offers expertise with upper limb external or body powered prostheses, myoelectric control, control site selection, socket interface design, beginning ADL training, trouble shooting, and more.
